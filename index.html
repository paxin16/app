GAME DESCRIPTION
Burger Toss is a free mobile endless runner game. You are the last burger alive and all you have to do is survive. Jump, super-jump, and gently guide yourself back to earth but the click of your mouse, or the tap of your finger. In Burger Toss you'll be doing a lot more sliding and gliding than just simple tossing. Your objective is to survive for as long as possible without running into the bluebird of misery or the sentient soda cans, fry boxes, and steel pillars that stand in your way. Collect pickles, tomatoes, lettuce, buns, and various other condiments in order to build your self a better, faster, stronger burger later on. Pick up a habanero pepper to become invincible and really show those side-orders whos boss. Burger Toss is an adorable and fast-paced free endless runner game where you can become the biggest mac of them all or die like a small fry. Try your luck today and make it your way at Burger Toss.

INSTRUCTIONS
On a desktop computer, use the left mouse button to jump. Press and hold the Left Mouse button and then release to jump even higher. While in the air press the left mouse button to fall back to earth fast. On a mobile device tap your finger to jump, tap-hold and release for a long jump, and tap while in the air to fall back to the platform.
